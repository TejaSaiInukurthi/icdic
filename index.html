    About the Institution


    Sree Vidyanikethan Engineering College (Autonomous), Tirupati was established in 1996 by Sree Vidyanikethan Educational Trust under the stewardship of Dr. M. Mohan Babu, renowned film artiste, Padma Shri Awardee and former Member of Parliament (Rajya Sabha).
    The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur, Anantapuramu, Andhra Pradesh, India.
    The Institution is conferred ‘Autonomy’ by UGC, New Delhi. The Institution also bestowed with the title ‘Colleges with Potential for Excellence’ by UGC, New Delhi under XII Plan period UGC-CPE scheme.
    The Institution was accredited with “A” grade by NAAC. The Institution received PLATINUM category in AICTE-CII Survey 2018.
    In NIRF Ranking-2019 Sree Vidyanikethan stands in 167th position among all Engineering Institutions at national level and topped among all the private engineering institutions in Andhra Pradesh.
    The Institution offers B.Tech. Program in 8 disciplines, M.Tech.Program in 8 specializations, Ph.D programs in 3 disciplines and MCA.Out of the programs offered, 6 B.Tech. and 4 M.Tech.
    programs were Accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA, New Delhi).
    The Institution was selected for participation in TEQIP-II under Sub Component 1.1: Strengthening Institution to improve learning outcomes and employability of graduates with a grant of Rs. 4 Crores. As one of the best performing Institutions under TEQIP-II, an additional fund of Rs. 2 Crores was sanctioned. The Institution has MoUs with 30 Industries / Institutions that includes IITH Hyderabad, IITM Chennai, CII Hyderabad, ISB Hyderabad.

    Location:

    The Institution is located 16 KM from the temple town of Tirupati on Tirupati – Madanapalle National Highway 205.


    Objectives of the Research Centers


    1. Data Analytics Research Center:


    • To predict high risk conditions in chronic diseases such as Diabetes and Heart-attacks using Data Analytics Tools.
    • To conduct advanced Research & Development in Data Science and Cloud Computing to solve the issues of Social Networks and Agriculture.
    • To conduct research on multi-criteria based decision making for cloud services.
    • To develop software based on Big Data for Societal Missions such as Orphanages, Old-age homes and Differently-abled persons and Welfare Associations using Data Analytics and Prediction Models.

    2. Center for Intelligent Computing:


    • To develop the fast and efficient Object Detection Algorithms with the Machine Learning Techniques for applications such as Image Retrieval, Surveillance, and Advanced Driver Assistance Systems.
    • To apply Soft Computing Techniques for Optimizing the Performance of Smart Applications.
    • To develop the Intelligent Systems in Medical Image Processing Aids for Professional Graders.
    • To develop the fast and efficient algorithms to enable the Intelligent Systems and Smart Technologies for the people.
    • To provide Security Solutions for huge Data Transactions and Analytics.

    3. Center for Computer Networks & Security:


    • To create awareness on Cyber Threats and train the users to safe guard their Infrastructure.
    • To conduct fundamental research on Computer Communications, Computer Networks, Parallel & Distributed Computing Systems, Wireless Networks, Sensor Networks and Cloud Computing.
    • To Design, Development and Analysis of new cryptosystem.
    • To conduct basic research on Intrusion Detection & Prevention.
    • To carry out Consultancy work on Malware Analysis, Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing.
